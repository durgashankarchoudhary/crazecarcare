About Us

Welcome to Craze Car Care, where we are passionate about revolutionizing the way you think about car cleaning and care. As a premier eco-friendly car wash and car servicing company, we bring all the car care solutions right to your doorstep. Based in Lucknow, we proudly extend our services to multiple cities through a vast partner network, encompassing 281 service stations across 16 cities in India.

Since our inception in 2017, Craze Car Care has been driven by a team of seasoned experts with over 10 years of Professional Car Washing & Car Servicing Experience. With a firm foothold in all locations across Lucknow, we strive to provide you with an unparalleled Car Washing & Car Servicing Experience, blending expertise, cutting-edge equipment, and best-in-class technology.

Our commitment to a Clean & Green Lucknow is unwavering, and as a testament to that, we employ modern water reclamation modules as an integral part of our car wash & car servicing systems. Moreover, all our products are biodegradable and eco-friendly, ensuring that your car is pampered without causing harm to the environment.

At Craze Car Care, we have redefined the car cleaning and servicing experience with a unique and conceptual approach. We pamper your cars with the latest equipment, including Organic Soaps, our effective & shiny INFINITY product line, spray injection, foaming machines, and the world's finest Castrol Engine Oil, leaving your vehicle spotless and rejuvenated.

Our highly skilled professionals are dedicated to taking care of every aspect of your car's interior & exterior, providing a service that exceeds your expectations. From washing to servicing, we deliver the finest craftsmanship with a personal touch.
